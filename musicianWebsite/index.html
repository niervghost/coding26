About Us

Oblivion was not built. It was summoned.

From the depths of distortion and the silence between storms, Oblivion exists where light surrenders and sound becomes something heavier than words. We do not play music. We excavate it — pulling it from the dark places most refuse to look, dragging it into the open where it can finally breathe

We were forged in the spaces between — between fury and stillness, between what is spoken and what is felt in the bones. Every riff is a confession. Every breakdown, a burial. Every moment of silence on stafe is a held breath before the world cracks open again.

This is not entertainment. This is a reckoning

Heavy metal has always been the music of those who refuse to be quiet about their pain. We carry that tradition like a wound that won't close — proudly, defiantly, without apology. Oblivion is not an scape from the darkness. It is an invitation to walk through it.

We are the noise in your chest at 3am. The weight you can't name. The end of something you were afraid to let go of. Oblivion is the moment before the fall — and the strange peace found at the bottom.

The void has a sound. You're already listening.

If you've found us, you already know why.
